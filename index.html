Frequently Asked Questions
Does my team need to install anything?+
No. RecapHQ connects directly to your existing Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet environment. Your team continues operating exactly as before. RecapHQ works in the background without requiring any change to established workflows.
Is our meeting data secure?+
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Your meeting content is never shared with or sold to third parties. It belongs exclusively to your organisation.
How long does setup take?+
Most organisations are fully configured and processing their first meeting within 30 minutes. The entire onboarding process takes place remotely via a single Zoom call at no additional cost.
Can I choose which meetings get recorded?+
Entirely. You retain full control over which meetings RecapHQ processes. Rules can be configured by meeting type, attendee list, or calendar category, or each meeting can be approved individually.
What if I want to cancel?+
You may cancel at any time with no penalty. Your full archive remains accessible for 30 days following cancellation, giving you sufficient time to export anything your organisation needs.
Does it work for in-person meetings too?+
Yes. Any audio file or transcript from an in-person meeting can be uploaded and processed through RecapHQ in exactly the same way, producing a full structured briefing, commitment log, and archived record.
